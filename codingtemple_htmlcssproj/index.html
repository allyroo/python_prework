Why Web Development?

Like many, I developed an interest in coding through Myspace. I saw that other people had super cool profiles, so I wanted to have a super cool profile! I poured in hours looking at other people's HTML layouts and messing with the code not really knowing what I was doing, but figuring out ways to make it look the way I wanted it to. My hobby became designing pages for friends.
I love puzzles and losing a sense of time trying to solve them; it is rewarding being able to have an idea of what you want to build and executing it. I am working to expand my skillset so that I can be a well-off travel lady $upporting my$elf while doing work that is enjoyable and meaningful to me!
